Soldier of the Old War

Former member of Egypt's elite Mukhabarat, Husayn is a Tier 1 trained operator. In late 2010, after a classified operation in the South Sinai fighting the Islamic State went bad, he left the Special Operations community, becoming, instead, a high school professor. After Unification Day, and still haunted by the ghosts of his past, he attempted to stay out of the occupation, but, as more and more of his students and their families go missing, the Nightmare of Eilat has taken up arms once more...
